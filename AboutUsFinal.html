Know a little more about us!

Alemans Grooming Salon is a family based salon that was opened in December of 2018 to better the experiences of your pets!. We have heard of horror stories of puppies that sadly are treated into submission to be able to groom them and that only causes anger, scaredness, and tramua to the pet. We have learned that patience and love go a long way as well. The values of our groomers are honesty, kindness and trust. Not only do we need trust with the pet, but as well as the owner. We will walk you through the entire process as well as make you aware of any triggers your puppy may have to educate you and work at home with those triggers.
