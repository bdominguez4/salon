About us

This business began in December of 2018 to change grooming experiences for pets. This includes dogs and cats of any breed. Being gentle and loving is the way to improve a pets experience and have them actually enjoy grooming. Our clientle grows more as years go by because clients are able to see a diffence in their pets behavior when they arrive to our salon. Our core values will always be honesty, kindness, and trust. Having happy pets keep our hearts going as well as the dedication to our jobs.

How Do We Work?

We are an appointment only business to ensure that every pet has their reserved time to get in and out to minimize their stress levels. We are also a no kennel salon.
