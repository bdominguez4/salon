Services Offered

All of our services are created with the intent to service the needs of each unique breed and health condition. With our limited packages, we address the basic grooming needs of all our furry little friends and more. With these purposeful options, we strive to strengthen their well-being every time a pet comes through our doors. All services are priced based on the breed & size of dog, length & condition of coat, frequency of grooming, and temperament of dog.

All of the services include:

  • Full groom
  • Blowouts
  • Nail Trimming
  • Ear Cleaning
  • Teeth Cleaning
  • Anal Gland Expression

Add-Ons to service include:

  • Dematting
  • Deshedding
  • Deodorizing
  • Whitening
  • Oatmeal
  • Flea Treatment
  • Conditioner
  • Medicated shampoo
  • Hydrating Shampoo

Policies:


No call No show appointments are entitled to a $25 dollar fee that will have to be paid before any other service can be done.

We offer a 15 min grace period, after that we do have a $15 fee if you would like to have your puppy done that day.

We offer 1 hour for pick up after we give you a call to inform you that your puppy is ready. After this hour we do have a $25 fee for additional care.

Any picks up after 5pm, which is closing time will be entitled to a $50 fee.
